About

Making things
feel right.

I'm a designer and engineer with six years of experience building products, systems, and art that people connect with. I started in sculpture and print, moved into digital design, and never fully left either world.

My work is driven by a belief that the best products are the ones where craft is invisible — where the engineering and the aesthetic feel like they were born together, not bolted on after the fact.

When I'm not at a screen, I'm in the studio: casting resin, shooting film, or working on the next generative series.
